Top Movies of All-time According to Sir Tanmay Mudgal.

Top 10 Movies To Watch:

1. Shanghai Knights

This movie stars Jackie Chan and goes about an adventurous journey.

2. Tenet

This movie is based on the concept of time. It is a mind-bending thriller.

3. Inception

This movie's plot is fascinating. It is an action-thriller. Must watch.

4. Interstellar

This movie is based on space travel.

5. Insideous

This is a horror movie series. Must watch if you are interested in horror genre.
